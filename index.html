Interactive COVID-19 Simulation

This simulation is not meant to be a realistic representation of the spread of COVID-19, but a simplified model meant to encourage critical thinking. Feel free to experiment with the parameters and see how they affect the spread of the virus.

Quick guide

Gray nodes are healthy.
Red nodes are infected.
Green connections are family.
Purple connections are friends.
Blue connections are people from the same school/workplace.
Gray connections are strangers.

Node count - number of people in the simulation. If the slider is set to 0, a random number of nodes will be generated.
Infected percentage - how many people are infected at the start of the simulation.
Virus aggressiveness - base of how likely the virus is to spread from one person to another.
Restrictions - different measures that affect the spread of the virus. Hover over them to get a basic idea of their effect.
Scenarios - predefined sets of restrictions and virus aggressivity that might be interesting to compare.
Start/Stop - start and stop the simulation.
